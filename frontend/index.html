Our Story

A legacy of
innovation since 2008.

CISETA — the Computer and Information Science Engineering Technical Association — was founded in 2008 by the Department of Computer Science at Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering, Bangalore. What began as a modest inter-department tech quiz has grown into one of Bangalore's most anticipated college tech fests.

Each year, CISETA unites engineering students from across Karnataka to compete, collaborate, and celebrate the world of computing — from AI hackathons and algorithm sprints to paper presentations and creative coding challenges.

CISETA 2025 marks the 17th edition of this celebrated tradition — bigger, bolder, and more competitive than ever before.
